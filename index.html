ABOUT HAVANA

Havana Company is one of the major sellers of tobacco products throughout the USA.

With several tobacco factories and rich history, for 50 years we have been manufacturing and supplying the best cigars and premium tobacco to America. We sell our cigars to the US shops of any size and to individuals through the chain of Havana Tobacco stores.

PARTNERS

The excellence of our premium tobacco has been evaluated by our partners:

15 Sorts of high-quality tobacco cultivated and dried at our own factories.
150 Acres of plantations in Cuba, as well as 5 tobacco processing factories.
80 Havana official stores in the USA, and 1500+ resellers all over the world.

HISTORY

For over 50 years, we have been engaged in tobacco growing and the production of cigars and other tobacco products.

SEPT 19, 1970

Foundation of our first tobacco factory in Havana by three brothers: James, John and Hugh Bomstrom.

JUN 2, 1990

Rise to the market leader in the USA, the opening of the first official store and 3 more factories.

NOV 17, 2000

Signing a contract with Trader Joe's. Since then, we've become a regular supplier to all its stores.

MAY 5, 2015

Establishment of the fifth tobacco factory in Cuba. Market launch of 3 new cigar brands.

OUR BRANDS

We sell tobacco products under several subsidiary brands. Look for these brands in stores in your city.

FINE CIGAR

Imported from Cuba, these machine-made cigars use select natural leaf wrappers and aged filler tobaccos to emphasize their nice flavor and aroma. Produced since 2000, this brand included in the Havana family has a huge following among American smokers.

CIGAR WORLD

Handmade in Cuba, the Cigar World cigars display the finest aged binder and filler tobaccos surrounded by natural wrapper for great flavor with that special Havana touch. The rich tobacco sweetness is accentuated with hearty notes of dark roast coffee.

CIGAR BAR

Cigar Bar cigarillos are hand-rolled in Cuba with natural leaf wrappers. Choose the Aroma version dipped in cognac, the Sweet Filtered cognac-dipped or the strong Rum version. Allow yourself an evening with good tobacco.
